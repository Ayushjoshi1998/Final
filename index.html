Glaciers in the Garhwal Himalaya in India are retreating so fast that researchers believe that most central and eastern Himalayan glaciers could virtually disappear by 2035. The map above shows the largest glaciers ( > 10 2km) in the tibetan plateau that are at risk of disappearing.

Coastal Cities are already seeing increased flooding

40% of the human population live within 100km of the coast and even small increments of sea level rise can put countless people at risk. The melt water from glacier melting in addition to increased rains in coastal areas may make these areas inhospitale in the future.
